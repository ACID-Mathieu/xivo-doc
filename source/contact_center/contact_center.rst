**************
Contact Center
**************

Introduction
============

In XiVO, contact center is implemented to full fill the folowing objectives :

* Call routing

  Includes basic call distribution using call queues and skills-based routing

* Agent and Supervisor workstation.

  provides ability to execute contact center actions as, login, logout and to receive
  real time statistics regarding contact center status

* Statistics reporting

  Provices contact center management reporting on contact center activities

* Advanced functionalities

  Call recording

* Screen Pop Up
